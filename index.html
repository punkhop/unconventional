Everybody nerds out about something.

Unconventional explores and celebrates the weird and wonderful world of conventions, expos, and gatherings. From mermaids to hackers to motorcyclists, every passion is investigated, celebrated, and playfully teased.

Each episode, host Peet Guercio goes from being an outsider to an insider as he is exposed to the unique and colorful people who aren't ashamed to pursue their passion.

In the current world of snarkiness and division, Unconventional seeks to remind us that exposure to new experiences and people is the best way to expand our minds, make new friends, and change our lives for the better.

Act Breakdown

Introduction

Peet introduces the audience to the culture of the week and we meet our expert/liaison. They teach Peet about the culture and establish his/her goals for the convention.

Fun and Games

Our expert gives Peet a crash course in their respective culture. Peet joins in and takes his first steps into becoming an honorary obsessive. The expert and Peet work toward the goal that was set in Act 1.

The Gathering

Peet and our expert go to the gathering together and experience all the sights and sounds of the culture. Peet and our liaison fight together to help the attendee succeed.

Wrap Up

An overview of what we saw and who we met, a farewell to the gathering, and an appeal to the audience to suggest why we should come to their favorite expo next.

Why this show?

Exposure

Seeing inside the lives and interests of other people is compelling – it’s why we know so much about ice road truckers and housewives. The fanatics love sharing their passion and viewers love to watch.

Inclusion

Unlike the conflict-driven narratives of other reality programming, positivity rules on Unconventional. All are welcome, accepted, and celebrated.

Comedy

Comedian host Peet keeps the laughs coming as he dives head first into each convention, activity, and interview.
